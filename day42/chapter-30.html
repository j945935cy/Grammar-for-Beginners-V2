單元四：問句與回答篇

Day 30｜there is / there are

這一章要學 there is 和 there are，讓你可以用英文說某個地方有什麼東西。

回首頁 前一天 下一天
學

本章學習目標

理解用途

知道 there is / there are 是用來表達「有」的句型。

分辨單複數

能分清楚單數用 is，複數用 are。

會寫問句

會寫肯定句、否定句和問句。

1

白話解釋

如果你想說某個地方「有」什麼東西，可以用 there is 或 there are。

基本概念

There is a book on the desk. 桌上有一本書。

There are two books on the desk. 桌上有兩本書。

後面如果是單數名詞，多半用 there is；如果是複數名詞，多半用 there are。

2

常見錯誤

錯誤 1：單數複數配錯

There are a dog in the park.

There is a dog in the park.

a dog 是單數，所以用 is。

錯誤 2：複數還用 is

There is three apples on the table.

There are three apples on the table.

three apples 是複數，所以用 are。

錯誤 3：問句順序錯誤

There is a cat under the chair?

Is there a cat under the chair?

問句要把 is / are 放到 there 前面。

3

正確句型

There is + 單數名詞 + 地點

There is a pencil in my bag.

There are + 複數名詞 + 地點

There are two boys in the room.

問句：Is / Are there + 名詞 ... ?

Is there a library near here? / Are there any chairs?

4

例句

  • There is a cat on the sofa. 沙發上有一隻貓。
  • There are many students in the gym. 體育館裡有很多學生。
  • There is some milk in the fridge. 冰箱裡有一些牛奶。
  • There are two buses at the stop. 站牌那裡有兩台公車。
  • Is there a bank near here? 這附近有銀行嗎？
5

練習題

練習說明：下面 5 題是改錯題，請把 there is / there are 句型改正。

  1. There are a book on the desk.
  2. There is three cats in the yard.
  3. There is a pencil in my bag.
  4. There are two girls in the room.
  5. There is many trees in the park.
答

答案與解析

  1. There is a book on the desk.

    解析：a book 是單數，所以用 is。

  2. There are three cats in the yard.

    解析：three cats 是複數，所以用 are。

  3. There is a pencil in my bag.

    解析：這題本來就是正確句子。

  4. There are two girls in the room.

    解析：這題本來就是正確句子。

  5. There are many trees in the park.

    解析：many trees 是複數，所以用 are。
