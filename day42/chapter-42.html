1

白話解釋

複合句是由一個主句和一個或多個從屬子句組成；倒裝句是把句子順序調整，強調句型則用特殊結構把重點放在要強調的部分。

複合句例子

I will go if it does not rain.

強調句型例子

It is my sister who won the prize.

2

常見錯誤

錯誤 1：從屬子句缺少連接詞

I will go I finish my homework.

I will go if I finish my homework.

錯誤 2：特殊問句順序錯誤

Where you are going?

Where are you going?

錯誤 3：強調句型用錯結構

It is him who is good at math.

It is he who is good at math.

3

正確句型

if + 主詞 + 動詞, 主句

If it rains, we will stay home.

when / because / although + 子句

Because it is cold, I wear a coat.

特殊問句：疑問詞 + be / 助動詞 + 主詞

Why are you late?

強調句型：It is + 強調部分 + that + 其他

It is my sister who helped me.

4

例句

  • When the bell rings, class will start. 當鐘響時，上課就開始。
  • Because he studied hard, he passed the exam. 因為他用功，他考試及格。
  • Why did you come late? 你為什麼遲到？
  • Never have I seen such a difficult exam. 我從未看過這麼難的考試。
  • It is the teacher who helps us learn English. 是這位老師幫助我們學英文。
5

練習題

練習說明：請把下面簡單句改成複合句、特殊問句或強調句型。

  1. I finished my homework. I can watch TV.
  2. She is the one who won the prize.
  3. Why you don't come?
  4. I have never see that movie.
  5. It is my friend who called me.
答

答案與解析

  1. If I finish my homework, I can watch TV.

    解析：用 if 子句連接兩句話。

  2. It is she who won the prize.

    解析：用 it is ... who 強調得獎的人。

  3. Why didn't you come?

    解析：特殊問句要把助動詞放在主詞前。

  4. Never have I seen that movie.

    解析：否定開頭使用倒裝。

  5. It is my friend who called me.

    解析：強調句型用 it is … who 表示強調。
