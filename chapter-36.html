Chapter 36｜關係子句

這一章要學關係子句，幫助你用 who / which / that 連接兩個句子。

回首頁 上一章 下一章
學

本章學習目標

認識關係子句

知道關係子句用來說明名詞。

分辨關係代名詞

學會何時用 who、which、that。

連接句子

把兩個簡單句合成一個完整句。

1

白話解釋

關係子句可以讓你把描述名詞的句子接在名詞後面，讓句子更完整。

簡單說法

This is the girl who sings well. 這就是那個唱得很好的人。

I like the book that you gave me. 我喜歡你給我的那本書。

基本結構是：名詞 + who / which / that + 子句。

2

常見錯誤

錯誤 1：少寫關係代名詞

The boy plays soccer is my brother.

The boy who plays soccer is my brother.

錯誤 2：關係代名詞用錯

I have a dog which can speak.

I have a dog that can speak.

錯誤 3：句子順序錯亂

The girl is my friend who dances.

The girl who dances is my friend.

3

正確句型

名詞 + who + 子句

The boy who studies hard is my friend.

名詞 + which + 子句

The book which I read was interesting.

名詞 + that + 子句

The car that he bought is red.

4

例句

  • She is the girl who sings well. 她就是那個唱得很好的人。
  • This is the pen which I lost. 這就是我弄丟的那支筆。
  • He likes the song that sounds happy. 他喜歡那首聽起來快樂的歌。
  • The teacher that teaches us is kind. 教我們的老師很親切。
  • I know the boy who can swim fast. 我認識那個會游很快的男孩。
5

練習題

練習說明：請把下面句子改成用關係子句的句型。

  1. The woman is my mother. She works at a school.
  2. The cake looks nice. It was baked by my sister.
  3. The boy is my friend. He plays basketball.
  4. That is the book. I read it yesterday.
  5. The dog is friendly. It belongs to my neighbor.
答

答案與解析

  1. The woman who works at a school is my mother.

    解析：用 who 連接描述母親的子句。

  2. The cake which was baked by my sister looks nice.

    解析：用 which 連接 cake 與被動子句。

  3. The boy who plays basketball is my friend.

    解析：who 後面接 plays basketball。

  4. That is the book that I read yesterday.

    解析：用 that 連接 book 與 read yesterday。

  5. The dog that belongs to my neighbor is friendly.

    解析：用 that 連接 dog 與 belongs to my neighbor。
