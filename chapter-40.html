Chapter 40｜間接引語

這一章要學把直接引語改成間接引語，並注意時態、代名詞和時間詞的變化。

回首頁 上一章 下一章
學

本章學習目標

了解間接引語

知道如何用 that 或省略 that 來改寫別人說的話。

注意時態變化

學會把現在式改成過去式，把 will 改成 would。

調整代名詞與時間詞

知道不同主語和時間詞需要一起改成間接語氣。

1

白話解釋

間接引語就是把「她說：I am happy」改成「她說她很高興」，不直接引用原句，並且調整人稱和時態。

基本轉換

She said, "I am happy." → She said that she was happy.

2

常見錯誤

錯誤 1：不改時態

He said he is tired.

He said that he was tired.

錯誤 2：忘了改代名詞

She said that I am hungry.

She said that she was hungry.

錯誤 3：時間詞沒改

He said that he will go tomorrow.

He said that he would go the next day.

3

正確句型

現在式 → 過去式

She said that she was happy.

can → could / will → would

He said that he could come.

I → he / we → they

She said that he was busy.

4

例句

  • She said, "I am tired after class." → She said that she was tired after class.
  • He said, "I can finish my homework." → He said that he could finish his homework.
  • My mom said, "We will go to school tomorrow." → My mom said that they would go to school the next day.
  • Tom said, "I have finished my homework." → Tom said that he had finished his homework.
  • She said, "This lesson is interesting." → She said that the lesson was interesting.
5

練習題

練習說明：請將下列直接引語改寫成間接引語，注意時態、代名詞與時間詞的變化。

  1. He said, "I am tired."
  2. She said, "I can speak English."
  3. My father said, "We will go tomorrow."
  4. Tom said, "I have finished my homework."
  5. She said, "This is my book."
答

答案與解析

  1. He said that he was tired.

    關鍵：時態後退 → am 變 was。

  2. She said that she could speak English.

    關鍵：can → could。

  3. My father said that they would go the next day.

    關鍵：will → would，tomorrow → the next day。

  4. Tom said that he had finished his homework.

    關鍵：現在完成式退後為過去完成式。

  5. She said that that was her book.

    關鍵：this → that，is → was；注意 that 在間接引語中常保留。
